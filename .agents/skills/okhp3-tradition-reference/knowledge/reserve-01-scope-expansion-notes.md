# Reserve 01 -- Phase 2 Scope Expansion Notes

This is a reserved knowledge scaffold for future content related to potential Phase 2 scope expansion beyond the current three in-scope Abrahamic traditions.

## ARE Phase 1 scope (current)

Three traditions meet both qualifying criteria:
1. Abrahamic lineage
2. 1% or greater US population (Pew Research Center)

| Tradition | US Share | In scope |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Christianity | ~63% | YES |
| Judaism | ~2% | YES |
| Islam | ~1% | YES |

## Phase 2 prerequisites

Before expanding scope to new traditions, the following conditions should be met:
1. A decision to revise the scope criteria (e.g., drop the Abrahamic lineage requirement)
2. A free, anonymous public API available for the new tradition's primary texts
3. A governance review of AGENTS.md Section 8
4. Proportional representation recalibration across all existing content
